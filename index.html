About Homeless Aid UK

Homeless Aid UK is an organisation established and operated by a group of friends from across the UK. Please note we are not a charity. Our team of volunteers are dedicated to ending homelessness, by delivering life-changing services and campaigning for change.

We rely solely on donations and the support of the local community.

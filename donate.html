Homeless Aid UK

Every Penny
Goes Direct

We're 100% voluntary and receive no funding. No salaries. No corporate overhead. When you give to us, your money goes straight to feeding and helping people.

We're not a registered charity — we're a group of friends who give a damn. That means no Gift Aid or tax receipts, but it also means zero bureaucracy between your donation and someone getting a hot meal.

Where Your
Money Goes

Straight to the people who need it. Here's what your donation pays for.

Food & Meals

Providing hot meals and food packages to those in need

Clothing

Essential clothing and blankets for warmth and dignity

Essential Items

Toiletries, first aid supplies, and hygiene products

Outreach

Transportation and equipment for our street teams

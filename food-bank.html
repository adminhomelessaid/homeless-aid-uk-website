How It Works

1

Call Us

Ring our freephone number 0800 124 4641 and let us know you need a food parcel.

2

We Arrange a Time

We'll book you in for a collection slot between 11am and 1pm, Monday to Saturday.

3

Come & Collect

Visit us at SureStore, Orlando St, Bolton, BL2 1DU at your booked time and pick up your parcel.

Food parcels are by appointment only. Please don't turn up without calling first — we want to make sure we have a parcel ready for you.

What's In Your Parcel

We have put together a hamper filled with everyday essentials, and a few treats because we care.

Essential Items

Tinned goods, pasta, rice, cereals, and other long-lasting food staples

Fresh Items

Bread, milk, and fresh produce when available

Treats & Extras

Small treats, snacks, and comfort items because we care

Important Information

Because of the scale of this project, everyone is receiving almost the same contents in the food hampers and therefore not all food may be suitable for you. Please check the contents of the hampers carefully if you have any medical, or religious, dietary requirements.

We're Here to Help

Emergency Food Parcels

For families in immediate need of food assistance. Call us and we'll get you sorted as quickly as we can.

Family Support

Tailored support for families with children. We'll do what we can to make sure your family is looked after.

Additional Services

Information about other local support services available. If we can't help directly, we'll point you to someone who can.

Should you have any queries or require any further information please do not hesitate to contact any of the team at Homeless Aid UK.

Not in Bolton?

We run street kitchens and outreach across northern England and Scotland. There might be a free meal near you today.
